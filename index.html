Doraemon gadgets are futuristic devices created by a robotic cat named Doraemon. They assist Nobita, the protagonist, in various aspects of life, including transportation, academics, sports, and combat. From the Anywhere Door to the Time Machine, these gadgets bring excitement and adventure to the series.
